Overview

What is my-app?

An on-the-fly-ever-incomplete-but-useful application built for the purpose of a course on developing with awesome tools such as Ionic and Angular.

Why should I use my-app?

Here's a few reasons:

  1. To learn how to develop with Ionic and Angular, and other such web frameworks.
  2. To learn how not to develop.
  3. Reasons one and two.

Signup

How do I sign up?

On the login page, locate the purple 'signup' button. Clicking this will take you to a form which once submitted will make you a fully fledged member of the my-app club!

Is my-app free?
See the question 'Do I have to pay to use my-app?'.
